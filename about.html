keybored: online typing speed game

keybored is a typing speed game that tests your speed and accuracy. It's a simple game, but it's a great way to practice typing and improve your speed and accuracy. It's also a great way to waste time.

gamemodes:

  • Time trial: Type as many words as you can in 30 seconds.
  • Sprint: Type a 35 word paragraph as fast as you can.
  • Alphabet: Type the alphabet as fast as you can. (under development)
  • Pi: Type as many digits of pi as you can remember. (under development)
